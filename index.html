I'm not sleeping well but I don't have a diagnosis. Is this relevant to me?
This book was written precisely for that situation. The gap between "no diagnosis" and "not functioning well" is the territory this framework was built to navigate. Do I need any background in TCM or biology to use this?
No. Every concept is explained from first principles. TCM terminology is always grounded in physiological terms immediately after it's introduced. If you understand that your heart beats and your liver processes what you eat, you have sufficient background to follow everything in this book.
Can I use this alongside medication?
Yes. The protocol works with the body's own regulatory systems and does not interact with medication. However, if you are taking medication for sleep, anxiety, or cardiovascular conditions, be aware that the protocol may reduce your need for that medication over time. Discuss any changes with your prescribing clinician. This book does not constitute medical advice.
How quickly will I notice a difference?
The first noticeable shifts — easier sleep onset, reduced 3 AM waking, improved morning quality — typically appear within 7–14 days of consistent implementation. Deeper regulatory shifts take 4–8 weeks to consolidate. The protocol is not a treatment that ends. It is a relationship with your own biology that deepens over time.
